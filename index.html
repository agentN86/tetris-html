Controls

To move the block left, use the left arrow. When trying to move right, use the
right arrow. If your trying to make the block go down (to speed up the process),
be sure to use the down arrow. Don't worry if a game is too long, once your fine
with your score, just click the End Game button or Pause & End Game button.

How to Play

Get as much points (or your score) as possible. You must stack randomized blocks and try
not to get the blocks all the way to the top of the game board. When you get a complete
line, you will get points and the line will be deleted. Continue this until you lose. At
the bottom, you will see a "High Score". You can try to reach as much as possible.
The "next" shows what blocks comes next.

It is recommended to close the "Games" tab at the bottom of the page to refrain scrolling
to the bottom while using the down arrow. All you need to do is click the "Games" and it
should close. You can reopen it by clicking it again.
